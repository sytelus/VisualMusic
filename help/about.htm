About



  Visual Music is the program to create music using sound card's MIDI device. It also provides the language to write script for creating music called MScript.

Special Credits:

Arthur V. Edstrom whose program 'Visual Basic MIDI Piano', written in 1993, was the starting point for me. The piano like keyboard layout in Visual Music is indeed originally created by him. Unfortunately I do not have his email address or link to his pages.

The music albums 'The World Power' and 'Madman's Return' by Snap! and albums by Enigma were great inspiration for creating electronic music. The architecture of Visual Music and MScript was in part driven by their creations and wild imagination!
