MScript Features

  1. Very simple structure with least numbers of syntax rules.
  2. Anybody can write or extend compiler in almost no time!
  3. Every MScript command is extensible by adding more optional parameters.
  4. Supports 3 styles of comments.
  5. Supports short version for most of the command to quickly do trial and
    error.
  6. Gives you all elementary commands which are easy to grasp and still enough for full control!
  7. It's interpreted.
  8. Simultaneous run/control other scripts and share variables.
  9. Free flaw - delimit by space, tab or new line.
  10. On-fly-compilation - no binaries are produced at all! This is possible due to very easy structure and there's almost no time required in parsing.
