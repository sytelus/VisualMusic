Using InstrumentSelector

To Select instrument

Click on the instrument or press arrow keys.

To stop sample sound when selecting the instrument

Press on the % sign to turn on/off this feature.

To see favourite instruments

Press on the 4 to see favourite or all instruments.

To add instrument to favourite

Press on the ü to see favourite or all instruments.

To search for the instrument

Press on the y and type the letters that should be present in any of the word of instrument name
